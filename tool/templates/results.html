Results

WDSNum: WDS catalog number (also the index number)
WDSName: WDS name (set of coordinates)
WDS_disc: WDS discoverer code
components: For multiple component systems
(NaN if no additional components)
NOBS: Number of observations
LSTDATE: Year of last observation
LSTSEP: WDS last separtion in arcseconds
delta_sep: Change in separation (LSTSEP - FSTSEP)
delta_PA: Change in position angle (|LSTPA - FSTPA|)
delta_mag_GAIA: Delta magnitude calculated from GAIA magnitudes
(0.0 means no magnitude data)
STYPE: Spectral type
NOTES: Notes from the WDS

d_(component): Distance in parsecs by GAIA
px_prob: Percent probability of being close enough to be physical double
(calculated internally, 999 means no data)
dist_prob: Percent probability of being close enough to be physical double
(calculated by Richard Harshaw)
PM_prob: Percent liklihood of being binary based on proper motion
binary_prob: Percent probability of being binary (calculated by Richard Harshaw)
physical?: Binary guess (Richard Harshaw)
WDS_RA: RA from WDS
WDS_Dec Dec from WDS
gaia_PMRA(component): Proper motion RA by GAIA
gaia_PMDEC(component): Proper motion Dec by GAIA
gaia_mag_(component): Component magnitude by GAIA
